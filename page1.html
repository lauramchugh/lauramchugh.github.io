    “Syncing” for Navet "Dance and Music and Climate Change" event

    Collaborator: Helena

    Tools: SuperCollider, Soundgrain, NGIMU Motion Sensor

    “Syncing” is a sound and dance collaborative performance composed of sonifications of climate change data where dancer/choreographers use their bodies as instruments, generating sound through movement. By giving their actions a voice, which grows more powerful as the dancers reach synchronisation, Helena and I hoped to inspire in our audience a sense of agency, opportunity for cooperation and shared resilience in the face of catastrophes.
    Performed in Reaktorhallen at KTH as part of Navet’s 4-day endeavour to depict climate change data through sound and dance, in collaboration with SKH Dance and KMH.
